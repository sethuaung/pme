Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What makes this platform different from competitors?

We focus on deep local integration with Myanmar's financial ecosystem, including multiple mobile wallets and the MMQR standard. This allows for faster, more convenient payouts and a better user experience, often at a lower cost than traditional services.

How is my money and data kept secure?

Security is our top priority. We employ end-to-end encryption for all data, adhere to strict international standards like PCI/DSS and ISO 27001, and comply fully with the Central Bank of Myanmar's AML/CFT regulations to protect every transaction.

What is MMQR and how does it benefit me?

MMQR is Myanmar's universal QR code standard. Our integration means a sender doesn't need to know the recipient's specific bank or wallet. As long as the recipient's app supports MMQR, they can receive money instantly, making transfers more flexible and error-free.

Which mobile wallets can I send money to?

We are constantly expanding our network. Initially, we support major wallets like TrueMoney, with plans to rapidly onboard all financial institutions connected to the MMQR network. Check the app for the most current list of supported partners.
