What is Citizen Development?

Citizen Development empowers individuals without formal coding backgrounds to build business applications using low-code/no-code platforms. It bridges the gap between business needs and IT resources, accelerating innovation and efficiency.

This foundational course introduces PMI's methodology, providing a framework to ensure citizen-led solutions are built responsibly, securely, and in alignment with organizational goals.

Course Modules at a Glance

The course is structured into three focused modules, guiding you from fundamental concepts to strategic application development.

The Core Framework: Citizen Development Canvas

The Citizen Development Canvas is a key PMI tool that provides a structured guide for projects. It ensures all critical aspects are considered, from the initial idea to the final execution and value delivery.

Supercharging Ideas with Ideation 2.0

The course introduces Ideation 2.0, a concept that elevates simple brainstorming into a strategic process. It focuses on generating high-value, viable ideas that align directly with organizational strategy and are ready for rapid development.
