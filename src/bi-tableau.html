The Tableau Ecosystem

Tableau Desktop

The primary authoring tool. This is where you connect to data, perform analysis, and design visualizations and dashboards for your projects.

Tableau Server

A secure, collaborative platform for sharing and governing your dashboards within an organization. It's a private analytics hub for your team.

Tableau Public

A free platform to explore, create, and publicly share data visualizations online. A great way to build a portfolio and learn from the community.

Building Foundational Visualizations

Most Common Chart Types

Certain chart types are fundamental to data analysis. Bar charts excel at comparing categories, while line charts are perfect for tracking trends over time. Mastering these basics is the first step to effective storytelling with data.

Advanced Analytics: Calculated Fields

Go beyond your initial data. Calculated Fields allow you to create new metrics from your existing data, like calculating profit ratio from sales and profit. This is key to unlocking deeper, more customized insights.

SUM([Profit]) / SUM([Sales])

Example: Profit Ratio Calculation

Advanced Charts

Tableau offers a wide array of advanced charts to explore complex relationships, such as scatter plots to reveal correlations between variables or heat maps to visualize data density across a matrix.
