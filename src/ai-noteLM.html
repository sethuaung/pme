Sample Scenario: History Paper on the Roman Empire

Imagine you're writing a paper on the economic factors leading to the fall of the Roman Empire. Here’s how NotebookLM helps:

  1. Create a Notebook: Title it "Roman Empire Economy".
  2. Add Sources: Upload 5 academic PDFs, your professor's lecture notes (as a Google Doc), and a primary source text about Roman trade.
  3. Generate a Summary: Ask NotebookLM, "Provide a one-page summary of the key economic arguments across all sources." It instantly creates a detailed overview with citations from your documents.
  4. Ask Specific Questions: Query, "What did my sources say about the impact of inflation on the military?" NotebookLM pulls direct quotes and paraphrased points from multiple documents, saving you hours of searching.
  5. Brainstorm Ideas: Ask, "Generate a list of potential essay thesis statements based on these sources." You get several strong starting points for your paper.
  6. Create an Outline: Once you have a thesis, ask, "Create an essay outline based on the thesis 'debasement of currency was the primary driver of Rome's fall', using evidence from my sources."
