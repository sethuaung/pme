What is Business Intelligence?

Power BI is a suite of business analytics tools by Microsoft that lets you visualize your data and share insights. It transforms unrelated sources of data into coherent, visually immersive, and interactive reports and dashboards.

The Power BI Ecosystem

Power BI Desktop

The free authoring application for Windows. This is your canvas to connect, transform, model, and create visual reports from your data.

Power BI Service

The cloud-based (SaaS) service. Here you publish, share, and collaborate on reports, building dashboards and managing access for your team.

Power BI Mobile

Access your reports and dashboards on the go. View and interact with your data from anywhere on iOS, Android, or Windows devices.

Key Concepts for Deeper Insights

Data Modeling with DAX

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) is the formula language of Power BI. It allows you to create powerful, custom calculations called 'measures' that perform analysis on your data model. This is how you move from simple reporting to true business intelligence.

Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Revenue])

Example: A simple DAX measure to calculate total revenue.

Core Visualizations in Power BI

While Power BI offers hundreds of visuals, a few core types form the backbone of most reports. Understanding when to use each is crucial for clear communication. Bar and column charts are excellent for comparisons, while line charts track trends.
