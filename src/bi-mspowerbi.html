Module 1: Introduction to the Power BI Ecosystem

Power BI is more than just a single tool; it's a collection of software services, apps, and connectors that work together to turn your unrelated sources of data into coherent, visually immersive, and interactive insights.

Power BI Desktop

Your creation studio. A free Windows application where you connect to, transform, and visualize your data to build reports.

Power BI Service

Your collaboration hub. A cloud-based service (SaaS) used to publish, share, and collaborate on reports and dashboards.

Power BI Mobile

Your insights on the go. Native apps for iOS, Android, and Windows that allow you to securely access and view your reports anywhere.

Intro to DAX Measures

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) is a formula language for creating custom calculations. A "Measure" is a DAX formula that calculates a result from your data, like a sum or an average.

Total Revenue = SUM ( 'Sales'[OrderQuantity] * 'Sales'[UnitPrice] )

Modules 5 & 6: Visualizations & Interactive Dashboards

This is where your data becomes a story. Drag fields onto the report canvas to create visuals. The key is choosing the right chart for the job to make your data easy to understand.

Most Common Chart Types

Bar and column charts are excellent for comparing values across categories.

Making Reports Interactive

Static charts are good, but interactive reports are better. Power BI includes many features to let users explore the data themselves.

  • Slicers: On-canvas filters for users to narrow down data.
  • Drill-Through: Lets users navigate from a summary chart to a detailed page.
  • Cross-Filtering: Clicking a bar in one chart automatically filters all other charts on the page.
  • Bookmarks: Save specific states of a report page to guide users through a narrative.
