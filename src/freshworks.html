Deep Dive: Freshdesk

Freshdesk is a cloud-based helpdesk and ticketing system designed to empower support teams. It unifies conversations from multiple channels, automates workflows, and helps resolve customer issues faster, enhancing overall satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Freshdesk

How can I automate ticket handling?

Freshdesk allows you to set up automated workflows that prioritize and assign tickets based on custom rules, keywords, or sender details.

Can I create custom reports?

Yes, you can create and edit reports to track key metrics like ticket resolution time, agent performance, and customer satisfaction (CSAT) scores.

How can I customize the support portal?

Freshdesk allows you to customize the end-user portal's look, feel, and functionality with intuitive design choices and layout options, creating a unique support experience for your customers.

Who is responsible for the features customization process?

While customers have full control to customize their support portal and workflows, Freshworks provides dedicated customer support and implementation teams to guide them through the process, ensuring a smooth and effective setup.

Freshsales

How do I manage user profiles and permissions?

Freshsales provides detailed user management features that allow you to create, update, and manage user profiles and set permissions to control access to sales data.

What is a sales sequence?

A sales sequence is an automated series of emails, calls, and tasks designed to nurture leads and prospects over time, ensuring consistent follow-up.

Can I customize my CRM data fields?

Yes, you can create and edit custom fields for contacts and deals to capture and track unique data points that are specific to your business needs, allowing for a more tailored sales process.

Who is responsible for the features customization process?

The responsibility is a shared effort. Customers have the flexibility to create custom fields and sales sequences, while Freshworks' support and technical teams are available to provide assistance and expertise for more complex integrations or customizations.

Freshmarketer

How does Freshmarketer help with customer acquisition?

It helps by tracking visitor behavior, segmenting audiences for personalized campaigns, and running A/B tests to optimize landing pages and conversion rates.

How can I customize my marketing campaigns?

Freshmarketer enables you to customize marketing campaigns by using audience segmentation, personalized content, and behavioral triggers to send the right message to the right person at the right time.

Who is responsible for the features customization process?

Customers manage the campaigns and A/B tests. Freshworks' customer success team offers guidance and best practices to help customers customize their marketing workflows and achieve their desired results.

Freshchat & Freshcaller

What is unified messaging?

What customization options are available?

You can customize the chat widget's appearance, set up automated message flows, and define intelligent routing rules to ensure incoming chats and calls are directed to the most suitable agent.

The customer is responsible for setting up the chat widget and routing rules. However, Freshworks provides extensive documentation and a support team to assist with advanced configurations and ensure a seamless deployment.

Freshservice

What is ITSM?

ITSM, or IT Service Management, is a structured framework for managing IT services to ensure they meet the needs of the business. Freshservice simplifies this process for IT teams.

How can I implement self-service?

Freshservice helps you implement a self-service portal where employees can find solutions, submit IT tickets, and track the status of their requests without needing to contact an agent directly.

Can I customize the ITSM portal for my employees?

Yes, Freshservice allows you to customize the end-user portal by structuring the environment, defining portal layouts, and creating a service catalog to provide a seamless and intuitive experience for employees.

Who is responsible for the features customization process?

The customer's internal IT team is typically responsible for configuring the self-service portal and service catalog. Freshworks offers comprehensive support and professional services to help with initial setup and complex customizations.

Freshteam

What is the primary function of Freshteam?

Freshteam is an HR management solution that streamlines the entire employee lifecycle, from recruiting and onboarding new hires to managing employee data and offboarding.

How can I customize my hiring and onboarding workflows?

Freshteam allows you to customize hiring workflows to match your company's unique processes and create personalized onboarding checklists to ensure a smooth and consistent experience for new employees.

Who is responsible for the features customization process?

Customers have the freedom to customize their hiring and onboarding workflows. Freshworks' support team is available to assist with any technical questions and provide resources to help optimize the process for their specific organizational needs.
